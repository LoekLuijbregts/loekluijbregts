Who I am

My name is Loek Luijbregts. I'm a growth partner for founders and leaders — based in Tilburg, working across Europe. Nine years of building things without a clean job title left me with a rare combination of strategic instinct, hands-on execution capability, a wide network, and the ability to move faster than most organisations are used to.

I don't hand over advice and leave. I embed with founders and leadership teams, see what's stuck, and start moving. Sometimes the block is a decision that's been circling for months. The track record was built in marketing: 40+ SMB growth programmes, then nine years in cycling — 110M+ earned views without a euro of ad spend. The method travels anywhere an ambitious person wants a deeply desired change to actually happen. Which is most places worth working.

Some clients hire the marketing brain. Others hire the pacer next to them. The best give me room for both. Either way, things move faster than they did before I arrived.
