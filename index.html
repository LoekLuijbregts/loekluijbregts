Who I am

I've always moved
toward the real.
Away from the polished.

At 18 I went bikepacking alone — not for Instagram, but to know what I was made of when nobody was watching and the route got hard. As a student I trained for an Ironman in six months, learned to swim from scratch, and finished in 10:45. Not to win. To find out what happens when you commit to something too big and stay anyway.
