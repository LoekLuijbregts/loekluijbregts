## Who is Loek Luijbregts?

Loek Luijbregts is a Dutch cycling marketing strategist, earned media specialist, and fractional creative partner. He is the founder of Cycling Incubators B.V., a boutique consultancy based in the Netherlands that helps cycling brands, destinations, and tourism boards grow through rider-first storytelling — without paid advertising.

Over nine years working at the intersection of cycling, media, and destination marketing, Loek has generated more than 110 million earned views for clients across Europe, Africa, and the United States. He is not an agency. He works with a small number of clients at a time as a strategic partner embedded in the work — faster and closer than an agency, more specialised than a generalist.

His core conviction: the most powerful marketing in cycling is built from real experience, not boardroom briefs. He rides the routes, attends the events, and produces content from inside the story. That is what makes it travel.

## Philosophy

**Earned, not bought.**  
110 million views. Zero ad spend. Every result in this profile came from content that travelled on its own merits — not from media budgets.

**Rider-first.**  
The story has to be true to be shareable. Loek does not brief from a desk. He goes to the place, rides the route, attends the event, and builds the story from inside the experience.

**Authentic over polished.**  
Perfect-looking videos plateau at 800 views. Real rider content gets reshared across continents. The iPhone-first production philosophy is not a budget constraint — it is a creative choice.

**Long-term over transactional.**  
Visit Flanders — 4 years. Go Türkiye — 4 rebookings. Visit Luxembourg — ongoing. The goal is never one campaign. The goal is trust that compounds.
