From the road,
not the conference room.

Each issue starts with something I actually observed — on a ride, at a trade show, in a conversation with a client or a journalist. Then it follows the thread: what it means, what it implies for cycling brands and destinations, and what I'd do about it.

It's not a newsletter about marketing in general. It's specifically about earned media in cycling — the corner of the market where stories replace ad spend, and where being embedded in the culture is the competitive advantage.
