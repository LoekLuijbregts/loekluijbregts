How it works

A fractional partner,
not an agency retainer.

A fractional engagement means you get senior-level strategic and creative capability embedded in your team — without the overhead of a full-time hire or the account management layers of an agency. The work moves at the speed of a founder, with the judgment of someone who has done this for nine years across 40+ cycling brands and destinations.

Engagements run on a quarterly basis. We set the direction together, execute on what matters, and adjust as the picture changes. There's no hierarchy between what was agreed in the brief and what the situation actually requires. If something isn't working, we change it — without a change-order process.

The starting point is always a 30-minute call where I want to understand what you're trying to do, what's blocking it, and whether my specific experience is actually the unlock. If it isn't, I'll tell you that too.

Honest criteria

This is probably
not for you if...

Saving both our time matters. These are the genuine disqualifiers.

You need someone to execute a brief you've already written — rather than co-create the strategy from the ground up.
Your main measure of success is impressions or follower counts rather than outcomes like leads, placements, or market position.
You want polished, brand-safe production that avoids showing any reality of what cycling actually looks like on the road.
You're in a category where cycling is incidental to what you sell — not central to the brand identity you're building.
You need a full in-house team, not a strategic partner — you'd benefit more from a CMO hire than a fractional.
You need results in three weeks — earned media compounds over a quarter, not a sprint.
